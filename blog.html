Mastering Pasta Aglio e Olio

March 25, 2026

Pasta Aglio e Olio is one of the simplest yet most elegant Italian dishes. With just a few quality ingredients— pasta, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes—you can create a restaurant-worthy meal in under 15 minutes.

The key to perfection is low heat and patience. Rushing the garlic will burn it and ruin the dish. Instead, let it infuse slowly into the olive oil, releasing its flavour gradually.

Why Fresh Ingredients Matter

March 15, 2026

The difference between cooking with fresh and stale ingredients is night and day. Fresh garlic has a sharp, pungent bite that mellows beautifully when cooked. Old garlic tastes bitter and muddy.

Shop at local farmers' markets when possible. Your dishes will taste better, and you'll support local producers. Plus, seasonal ingredients are always fresher and more affordable.
