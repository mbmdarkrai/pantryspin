## Ingredients

- 1 lb (400g) spaghetti or linguine
- 6-8 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup extra virgin [olive oil](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=extra+virgin+olive+oil&tag=mbm1212-21)
- 1/2 tsp [red pepper flakes](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=red+pepper+flakes&tag=mbm1212-21) (adjust to taste)
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
- Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Equipment

- [Large pasta pot](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=large+pasta+pot&tag=mbm1212-21)
- [Wooden spoon for stirring](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=wooden+spoon+set&tag=mbm1212-21)
- [Colander](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=kitchen+colander&tag=mbm1212-21)
- [Non-stick frying pan](https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?k=non-stick+frying+pan&tag=mbm1212-21)

## Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare the Water
Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add a generous pinch of salt.

### Step 2: Cook the Pasta
Add the pasta to boiling salted water and cook according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

### Step 3: Infuse the Oil
While the pasta cooks, gently heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-low heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is fragrant but not brown.

### Step 4: Combine Pasta and Oil
Add the drained pasta to the pan with the garlic oil. Toss well to coat. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water.

### Step 5: Season and Serve
Taste and adjust seasoning. Top with parsley and Parmesan if desired. Serve immediately.
